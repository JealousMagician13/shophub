Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or log in with your email and password to complete your purchase.

What's the shipping time?

We typically process orders within 1-2 business days and ship them within 3-5 business days. Delivery usually takes 7-10 business days depending on your location.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

What's your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unused items in original packaging. Contact our support team for a return authorization.

How can I track my order?

After your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. Use this number to track your package on the carrier's website.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use SSL encryption and PCI compliance to protect your payment information. Your credit card details are never stored on our servers.
